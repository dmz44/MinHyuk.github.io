Selected Projects

Course Development for Advanced Robotics and Autonomous Systems

Aug 2025 - Dec 2025

  • Served as Doctoral Instructor Assistant for developing and running Robotics course in TXST.

  • Developed coursework with real robot, Turtlebot 3 with Open manipulator Arm, for students to learn robot programming in Simulator and with real hardware.

  • Introduced innovative and pioneering robotics course with hands-on robot intergration with latest Artificial Intelligence technologies including YOLO and Large Language Models, and hardware such as Hailo NPU and Jetson SOC.

Multiagent Aerial Systems

Feb 2023 - May 2024

  • On going research for graduate school.

  • Implemented of makespan-optimized motion planning algorithms considering inter-swarm aerodynamic interference. Implemented collision avoidance algorithms (APF, BVC, Stop and Go) on the Crazyflie platform.

  • Publications include Wind Field Modeling for Formation Planning in Multi-Drone Systems in respected international conference for robotics, ICRA.

Robotic Arm for Nuclear Environment

Nov 2023 - May 2024

  • Served as Lead Programmer implementing simulated environment and core algoirhtms during graduate research.

  • Utilized Blender 3D modeling, MoveIt library, and Gazebo to implement motion planning for a virtual robotic arm based on real nuclear reactor specifications.

  • Presented research poster on improving efficiency for robotic arm double null divertor inspection during international ICRA nuclear workshop conference.

Service Robot Development

Feb 2023 - Nov 2023

  • Participated in National Research Foundation, South Korea’s Funding project as a graduate researcher.

  • Designed an AI Companion Service Robot using ROS 1. Implemented human skeleton detection based on OpenPose and Jetson NX.

  • Improved human interactivity and user friendliness of robots with latest AI technology and collaboration with Design School in UNIST.

Smart 3D Printer Farm Development

Feb 2022 - Nov 2022

  • Served as a Project Leader responsible for system design and programming during UNIST AI Challengers Program.

  • Used Generative AI (Variational AutoEncoder) and Object Detection (Darknet YOLO) for automated 3D printer diagnostics.

  • Submitted and approved South Korean Patent and presented in AI Challengers Program, 2022.

Autonomous Drone Racing

Feb 2017 - Nov 2018

  • Led the UNIST Dronebot team building hardware and software for the ADR competition.

  • Constructed custom drones with carbon fiber and 3D-printed parts, and integrated control code with ROS, Optitrack, ZED Stereo Cameras, and Jetson TX2.

  • Completed real obstacle course as fourth place in IROS international competition, authored a section in a collaborative journal paper.
