Autonomous Aerial Systems

IROS 2018 Autonomous Racing Drone

Designed, fabricated, and integrated a custom drone to complete a physical obstacle course at IROS 2018, achieving 4th place in the international competition. Implemented the full perception stack (ROS1, Optitrack, ZED Stereo Cameras, Jetson TX2) using Visual Inertial Odometry (VIO) and Optical Flow for localization, and YOLO for obstacle detection. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GCwo89aNn6g

High-Precision Indoor Drone Show Swarm

Orchestrated a coordinated drone light show and research platform using multiple Crazyflie platforms. Achieved high-accuracy positioning using the Valve Lighthouse 2 tracking system. Developed a custom trajectory planning workflow by compiling Skybrush from source and integrating Blender.

Outdoor Survey Drone

Custom-built survey platform equipped with an RTK GPS module for precision positioning and a Jetson Xavier NX companion computer. Deployed for an actual search mission, running YOLO object detection at the edge to find stranded pedestrians in forested environments.

Autonomous Ground Robots

AI-Driven Mobile Manipulator

Integrated a TurtleBot3 and OpenManipulator with a Jetson Xavier NX for on-device AI inference. Developed a multimodal ROS 2 pipeline using Voice-to-Text for natural language command input and Large Language Models (LLMs) for high-level task reasoning. Achieved real-time object grounding and manipulation using YOLO and CUDA acceleration.

AI-Augmented 3D Printing Manufacturing System

Developed an automated 3D printer system for anomaly detection and fleet management. System leverages Generative AI (Variational Autoencoders) to identify undocumented printing errors and YOLO for classifying known failures. Received a South Korean Patent for the innovative system.

Service & Companion Robotics (HRI Focus)

‘Modle’ AI Companion Robot

Co-developed an elderly care service robot in collaboration with the UNIST Design Department. Engineered the software stack on NVIDIA Jetson Xavier NX, integrating OpenPose for human skeleton detection, Voice-to-Text for natural interaction, and SLAM for autonomous indoor navigation.

Sony Aibo

Utilized the Sony Aibo 2 quadruped as a research platform for human-companion interaction, deploying custom behavioral scripts via the web API interface.

Ground Field & Research Platforms (Legged & Wheeled)

Unitree Go2

Enabled custom secondary development on the Unitree Go2 platform. Optimized the quadruped for traversing difficult unstructured terrain while carrying heavy payloads, including external companion computing units. Currently in testing with a robotic arm.

Segway RMP

Configured and maintained a Segway RMP mobile base for heavy-duty payload research (including full-sized displays). Integrated an Intel NUC to handle system bring-up and high-level control.

Husky A200

Deployed a Clearpath Husky A200 for outdoor field research. Managed the integration of internal companion computers for ROS bring-up and autonomous navigation in rugged environments.

Autonomous Model Scale Cars

Developed autonomous driving algorithms on 1/10th scale remote control cars featuring Ackermann steering geometry. Implemented ROS 1 control stacks for autonomous vehicle research, such as autonomous parking lot research.

Turtlebot 4

Deployed TurtleBot 4 unit for research and teaching, utilizing the ROS 2 architecture for modern navigation and mapping tasks.

Simulation & Digital Twins

Nuclear Reactor Inspection Arm Digital Twin

Collaborated with Korean Fusion Energy to simulate a high-fidelity nuclear reactor environment in Gazebo. The simulated robot arm was modeled in URDF format, and complex motion planning was implemented using the MoveIt framework in ROS.

Simulated Racing Drone

Recreated a racing drone obstacle course and drone in Unreal Engine to serve as an autonomous drone testing platform. The simulated drone uses a PX4 SITL (Software-in-the-Loop) instance connected directly to the simulator for realistic control and trajectory testing. Also used in coursework as part of the Particle Filter Assignment.

Outdoor Aerial Platforms (VTOL & Others)

Heewing VTOL Aircraft

Built and piloted a Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL) aircraft powered by ArduPilot. Configured transition parameters for tilting rotors to enable seamless switching between vertical hover and horizontal forward flight. Holds both Unmanned Fixed Wing and Multicopter Drone License.

Fixed Wing Planes

Constructed and piloted fixed-wing aircraft using ArduPilot for long-endurance flight missions. Holds a Korean 4th Class Unmanned Fixed Wing Drone License.

Racing FPV Drone

First Person View Racing Drones powered by Betaflight. 1st class license for multicopters in South Korea. Holds a Korean 1st Class Unmanned Multicopter License.

Aerial Manipulation Drones

Gripper Drone

Developed custom PX4-powered drones equipped with an actuated gripper for aerial pick-and-place tasks. Presented during the UNIST AI Challengers Program 2021.

Magnet Drone

Developed custom PX4-powered drones equipped with magnetic relay mechanisms for aerial pick-and-place tasks using reinforcement learning. Presented during the UNIST AI Challengers Program 2023.
