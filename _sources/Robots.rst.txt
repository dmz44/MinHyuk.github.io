Ground Robots
-------------

Turtlebot 3 with Open Manipulator and Jeston NX
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Turtlebot 3 with Open Manipulator X arm powered by OpenCR, Jetson Xavier NX. Planning for robot arm done with ROS 2 and Moveit library. Capable of running Voice to Text recognition, Large Language Models, YOLO object detection algoirhtm and SLAM algoirhtms, accerated by CUDA.

.. image:: turtlebot3.jpg
   :width: 200px
   :alt: MinHyuk Park
   :align: center
   
Service Robot for Elderly
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Ground service robot with Jetson Xavier NX. Capable of running Voice to Text recognition, YOLO object detection algoirhtm and SLAM algoirhtms.

3D Printer Farm with AI Management
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Automated 3D printer with custom Octoprint firmware capable of remote management. Nvidia Jetson uses generative AI, Variable Autoencoder to detect nondocumented abnormalities, and YOLO for documented abnormalities. Received South Korean Patent.

.. image:: printer_farm.jpg
   :width: 200px
   :alt: MinHyuk Park
   :align: center
