Autonomous Aerial Systems 
-------------------------

IROS 2018 Autonomous Racing Drone
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Designed, fabricated, and integrated a custom drone to complete a physical obstacle course at IROS 2018, achieving 4th place in the international competition. Implemented the full perception stack (ROS1, Optitrack, ZED Stereo Cameras, Jetson TX2) using Visual Inertial Odometry (VIO) and Optical Flow for localization, and YOLO for obstacle detection.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GCwo89aNn6g

High-Precision Indoor Drone Show Swarm
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Orchestrated a coordinated drone light show and research platform using multiple Crazyflie platforms. Achieved high-accuracy positioning using the Valve Lighthouse 2 tracking system. Developed a custom trajectory planning workflow by compiling Skybrush from source and integrating Blender.

Autonomous Ground Robots
------------------------

AI-Driven Mobile Manipulator
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Integrated a TurtleBot3 and OpenManipulator with a Jetson Xavier NX for on-device AI inference. Developed a multimodal ROS 2 pipeline using Voice-to-Text for natural language command input and Large Language Models (LLMs) for high-level task reasoning. Achieved real-time object grounding and manipulation using YOLO and CUDA acceleration.

.. image:: turtlebot3.jpg
   :width: 200px
   :alt: MinHyuk Park
   :align: center

AI-Augmented 3D Printing Manufacturing System
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Developed an automated 3D printer system for anomaly detection and fleet management. System leverages Generative AI (Variational Autoencoders) to identify undocumented printing errors and YOLO for classifying known failures. Received a South Korean Patent for the innovative system.

Simulation & Digital Twins
--------------------------

Nuclear Reactor Inspection Arm Digital Twin
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Collaborated with Korean Fusion Energy to simulate a high-fidelity nuclear reactor environment in Gazebo. The simulated robot arm was modeled in URDF format, and complex motion planning was implemented using the MoveIt framework in ROS.

.. image:: simulated_arm.jpg
   :width: 200px
   :alt: MinHyuk Park
   :align: center
   
Simulated Racing Drone
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Recreated a racing drone obstacle course and drone in Unreal Engine to serve as an autonomous drone testing platform. The simulated drone uses a PX4 SITL (Software-in-the-Loop) instance connected directly to the simulator for realistic control and trajectory testing. Also used in coursework as part of the Particle Filter Assignment.
