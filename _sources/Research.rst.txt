Selected Projects
-----------------

Course Development for Advanced Robotics and Autonomous Systems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Aug 2025 - Dec 2025*

* Served as Doctoral Instructor Assistant
* Developed coursework with real robot, Turtlebot 3 with Open manipulator Arm, for students to learn robot programming in Simulator and with real hardware.
* Introduced innovative and pioneering robotics course introducing robot intergration with latest Artificial Intelligence technologies including YOLO and Large Language Models, and hardware such as Hailo NPU and Jetson SOC.

Multiagent Aerial Systems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2023 - May 2024*

* On going research for graduate school.
* Implemented of makespan-optimized motion planning algorithms considering inter-swarm aerodynamic interference. Implemented collision avoidance algorithms (APF, BVC, Stop and Go) on the **Crazyflie** platform.
* Publications include Wind Field Modeling for Formation Planning in Multi-Drone Systems in international conference for robotics, ICRA.

Robotic Arm for Nuclear Environment
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Nov 2023 - May 2024*

* Served as Lead Programmer. 
* Utilized Blender 3D modeling, MoveIt library, and Gazebo to implement motion planning for a virtual robotic arm based on real nuclear reactor specifications.
* Presented research poster on improving efficiency for double null divertor inspection with robotic arm during international ICRA workshop conference.

Service Robot Development
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2023 - Nov 2023*

* Participated in National Research Foundation, South Korea's Funding project. 
* Designed an AI Companion Service Robot using ROS 1. Implemented human skeleton detection based on **OpenPose** and **Jetson NX**.
* Improved human interactivity and user friendliness with latest AI technology and collaboration with Design School in UNIST.

Smart 3D Printer Farm Development
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2022 - Nov 2022*

* Served as Project Leader. 
* Used Generative AI (Variational AutoEncoder) and Object Detection (Darknet YOLO) for automated 3D printer diagnostics.
* Submitted and approved South Korean Patent.

Autonomous Drone Racing
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2017 - Nov 2018*

* Led the UNIST Dronebot team. 
* Constructed custom drones with carbon fiber and 3D-printed parts, and designed control systems using ROS, Optitrack, ZED Stereo Cameras, and Jetson TX2.
* Achieved fourth place in international competition, authored a section in collaborative journal paper.
