Selected Projects
-----------------

Course Development for Advanced Robotics and Autonomous Systems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Aug 2025 - Dec 2025*

* Served as Doctoral Instructor Assistant for developing and running **Robotics** course at TXST.
* Developed coursework with a real robot, Turtlebot 3 with Open manipulator Arm, for students to learn robot programming in the Simulator and with real hardware.
* Introduced innovative and pioneering robotics course with hands-on robot integration with the latest Artificial Intelligence technologies, including YOLO and Large Language Models, and hardware such as Hailo NPU and Jetson SOC.

Multi-agent Aerial Systems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2023 - May 2024*

* Ongoing research for graduate school.
* Implemented makespan-optimized motion planning algorithms considering inter-swarm aerodynamic interference, as well as collision avoidance algorithms (APF, BVC, Stop and Go) on the **Crazyflie** platform.
* Publications include Wind Field Modeling for Formation Planning in Multi-Drone Systems in a respected international conference for robotics, ICRA.

Robotic Arm for Nuclear Environment
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Nov 2023 - May 2024*

* Served as Lead Programmer, implementing a simulated environment and core algorithms during graduate research.
* Utilized Blender 3D modeling, MoveIt library, and Gazebo to implement motion planning for a virtual robotic arm based on real nuclear reactor specifications.
* Presented a research poster on improving efficiency for robotic arm double null divertor inspection during the international ICRA nuclear workshop conference.

Service Robot Development
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2023 - Nov 2023*

* Participated in research collaboration with the Design School in UNIST as a graduate researcher.
* Designed an AI Companion Service Robot using ROS 1. Implemented human skeleton detection based on **OpenPose** and **Jetson NX**.
* Improved human interactivity and user friendliness of robots with the latest AI technology and human interactivity research.

Smart 3D Printer Farm Development
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2022 - Nov 2022*

* Served as a **Project Leader** responsible for system design and programming during the UNIST AI Challengers Program.
* Used Generative AI (Variational AutoEncoder) and Object Detection (Darknet YOLO) for automated 3D printer diagnostics.
* Submitted and approved South Korean Patent, and presented in the AI Challengers Program, 2022.

Autonomous Drone Racing
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
*Feb 2017 - Nov 2018*

* **Led the UNIST Dronebot** team in building hardware and software for the ADR competition.
* Constructed custom drones with carbon fiber and 3D-printed parts, and integrated control code with ROS, Optitrack, ZED Stereo Cameras, and Jetson TX2.
* Completed a real obstacle course in fourth place in the IROS international competition, authored a section in a collaborative journal paper.
